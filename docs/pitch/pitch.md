# InboxCFO Pitch

Small businesses often manage invoices across email, spreadsheets, and bank portals. InboxCFO turns invoice emails into structured financial records and predicts near-term cash flow.

## Problem

Owners and operators need to know what money is coming in, what bills are due, and whether cash will be tight before payroll or vendor payments.

## Solution

InboxCFO combines email automation, LLM extraction, Supabase, and a Flutter dashboard to:

- Extract invoice details from incoming email.
- Track income and expense invoices in one database.
- Forecast cash flow into `cash_projections` by due date and pending status.
- Surface overdue invoices and upcoming cash gaps.

## MVP

The first MVP should prove the database, ingestion workflow, and dashboard read path with demo invoices and forecasts.
